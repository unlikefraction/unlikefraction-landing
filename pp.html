Privacy Policy

UnlikeFraction privacy policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01

UnlikeFraction builds small, focused products and experiences. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it across our websites and apps.

What we collect

  • Basic usage data such as page views, device type, and rough location (city-level) to keep the service reliable.
  • Account and contact details only when you choose to provide them, such as an email for updates or support.
  • Content you submit to our products, limited to what is required to provide the feature you are using.

How we use data

  • Operate and improve the service, including debugging and performance monitoring.
  • Communicate with you about product updates or support requests you initiate.
  • Protect against abuse, fraud, and security incidents.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted infrastructure providers only to run our services, and only under contracts that require data protection.

Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations. When data is no longer required, we delete or anonymize it.

Your choices

  • You can opt out of non-essential analytics where available.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data.

Contact

Questions or requests: team@unlikefraction.com
